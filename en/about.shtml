About Us

Midinote is a special notebook app for people who have the habit of recording.

Initially，Midinote was developed for ourselves to record anything we thought was interesting or important.

Who we are?

We are two freelancers, love sports, cooking, reading, traveling, music ... and also we love knowledge and love to record. Once, we were deep users of Evernote, after years of use, with increasing number of notes, we found we need spend a lot of time to organize our notes, or everything will end up with a mess. Therefore, we started to develop a new notebook app and created Midinote.

About the name: Midinote

The name comes from the philosopher Sartre's notebook.
In a biography of Sartre (Adieux: A Farewell to Sartre), he mentioned he accidentally found a notebook on a metro train, which is a blank notebook with alphabetical tabs. He used it to record his thoughts of philosophy, if one note he wants to write down starts with A, then he writes it on A's pages. This is how he organizes his notes. So, in this ordinary notebook, Sartre wrote down his famous thoughts of Existentialism. Sartre said there are some weird words on the notebook's cover: "Midi Suppositories". We hope our Midinote is such a notebook and it is waiting you to write down your amazing thoughts!
