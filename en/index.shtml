MIDINOTE, ANOTHER WAY TO TAKE NOTES

Only Evernote? We are different, we use totally tag-based system to organize your notes, to record your memory in a well-organized, brain-like and visual way.

SCROLL DOWN

Totally Tag-Based

Traditional apps use folders or notebooks to organize notes, like Evernote, this way requires you to spend tons of time to organize your notes, or everything will end up with a mess.

In Midinote, we use tag-based system to classify content, it brings you a quick, free and will-organized way to take notes.

Mindmap & Visualization

With time, your tags will grow up into a Mindmap tree of your own and you can recall your memory in a visual way.

Personal Knowledge-Base

In Midinote, every tag is an article, or, if you are familiar with Wikipedia, every tag is an entry of encyclopedia, you can browse and jump back and forth between tags, just like you do in web browser.

With Midinote, you are building a personal knowledge base.

Support 100% Off-line Use

You can use all the Midinote functions at anytime and anywhere , even without Internet connection, actually, all the data will be synced from cloud to your device, and, Midinote promise never to analyze user's private data.
